Pro Tips

Essential Tips for Drivers

Make your airport experience smooth with these practical tips.

1

Arrive Early

For terminal parking, arrive 2 hours before your flight. For off-site parking with shuttle service, add 30-45 extra minutes to account for transfer time and check-in.

2

Book in Advance

Official airport parking can be booked online at least 6 hours ahead. Pre-booking often secures better rates and guarantees your spot during peak travel periods.

3

Fuel Up Before Parking

Fill your tank before heading to the airport ‚Äì fuel stations near the airport are convenient. This way your car is ready when you return from your trip.

4

Save Your Parking Location

Take a photo of your parking spot number and row. Large car parks can be confusing after a long trip. Some parking apps let you save your location.

5

Check for Discounts

Many off-site parking operators offer early booking discounts, loyalty programs, or promo codes. Check their websites or sign up for newsletters for deals.

6

Know the Shuttle Schedule

Off-site parking shuttles usually run 24/7 and track your flight. Call them when you land ‚Äì they'll pick you up within 5-10 minutes at the designated meeting point.
